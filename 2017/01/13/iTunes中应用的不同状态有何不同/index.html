不同的应用程序状态意味着什么？
准备提交：你创建了一个新版本的应用程序，现在你需要提供的应用程序的元数据，截图，定价信息，等等。
等待上传：您提供了所有所需的元数据，现在您可以将二进制应用程序的应用程序加载到应用程序商店。
无效的二进制：我们接收和处理您的二进制，但它没有满足我们的所有上传要求。有可能是一个与图标不满足我们要求的问题，层级设置错误,版本不是build的递增版本。不管是什么问题，不过，我们将向您发送一封电子邮件，详细介绍如何解决问题。
缺少截图（iOS）：你的应用程序是一个或多个应用程序的语言缺少所需的截图。通用的应用程序，不要忘了为iPad和iPhone提供屏幕截图。
等待审查：您提交您的二进制应用程序商店，它等待应用程序审查。
等待出口合规：你CCATS文件正在审查出口合规。
审查：您的二进制正在审查由应用程序审查。你可以把你的二进制码的应用程序审查队列通过单击拒绝二进制，而它有这样的状态。
待定合同：您的协议尚未敲定。您的应用程序将无法在应用程序商店，直到您的协议是完整的。
等待开发者发布：您的应用程序已通过审核，但在应用商店还不可用。因为您选择了使用版本发布控制，您的应用程序版本将无法使用，直到您点击发送版本。在我的应用-应用程序的版本信息窗格中执行这个操作。
应用程序商店的处理：您的应用程序版本正在处理，以便它可以成为应用程序商店准备。处理完成后，您将移动准备出售。
准备出售：您的应用程序已准备出售的应用程序商店指定的日期在应用程序的定价。如果指定的日期是在过去，该应用程序将立即在App Store上可用。要删除您的应用程序从一个地区出售，使用定价删除应用程序商店领土。
拒绝：我们无法批准您提交的二进制。如果你想提交一个纠正二进制，开始提交过程再次点击准备提交二进制。你会发现它在你的应用程序的版本信息窗格在我的应用程序。
元数据被拒绝：我们无法批准您的元数据。我们会送你一张带拒绝细节的建议。编辑我的应用程序中的元数据来解决这个问题。您的提交二进制将被重用的审查过程后，您的元数据已被固定。
开发人员拒绝：你从审查过程中删除了自己的应用程序。应用程序的审查过程将在你提交你的二进制重启。
从出售删除：我们需要从应用程序商店删除您的应用程序。要找出为什么你的应用程序被删除，只要问我们。
开发商删除出售：您删除您自己的应用程序出售的应用程序商店。
等待苹果发布：我们会保留您的应用程序版本，直到相关的iOS或OS X版本公布于众。
（待定应用程序传输）：这是一个次要状态，显示后的主要应用程序状态。目前的应用程序拥有者发起了一个应用程序的传输，但收件人还没有接受它。当前的应用程序拥有者和接收方都有能力取消转让。虽然应用程序有这种状态，没有改变，可以应用程序除了权利和定价。
（处理APP转账）：这是次要状态，显示后主要应用状态。收件人已经接受了应用程序传输。转移不能在这一点上取消，并没有改变，可以应用程序，直到转让完成。

What do the different app statuses mean?
Prepare For Submission: You created a new version of your app and now you need to provide the app’s metadata, screenshots, pricing information, and so on.

Waiting for Upload: You provided all the required metadata and now you can deliver the binary for your app to the App Store using Application Loader.
Invalid Binary: We received and processed your binary, but it didn’t meet all of our upload requirements. There might be an issue with the icon not meeting our requirements, the payload directory being placed at the wrong level in the .app wrapper, or a non-increasing CFBundleVersion being used. No matter what the issue is, though, we’lll send you an email with details on how to resolve the issue.
Missing Screenshot (iOS only): Your app is missing the required screenshots for one or more of your app’s languages. For universal apps, don’t forget to provide screenshots for both iPad and iPhone.
Waiting for Review: You submitted your binary to the App Store and it’s waiting for App Review.
Waiting for Export Compliance: Your CCATS file is being reviewed for export compliance.
In Review: Your binary is being reviewed by App Review. You can take your binary out of the App Review queue by clicking Reject Binary while it has this status.
Pending Contract: Your agreements haven’t been finalized. Your app won’t be available on the App Store until your agreements are complete.
Pending Developer Release: Your app version was approved by App Review but it’s not available on the App Store. Because you chose to use Version Release Control, your app version won’t be available until you click Send Version Live. Do that in the app’s Version Information pane in My Apps.
Processing for App Store: Your app version is being processed so it can become ready for the App Store. After processing is complete, you’ll move to Ready for Sale.
Ready for Sale: Your app is ready for sale on the App Store on the date specified in the app’s Pricing. If the specified date is in the past, the app will be immediately available on the App Store. To remove your app from sale for a territory, use Pricing to remove the App Store territory.
Rejected: We weren’t able to approve your submitted binary. If you want to submit a corrected binary, start the submission process again by clicking Ready to Submit Binary. You’ll find it on your app’s Version Information pane in My Apps.
Metadata Rejected: We weren’t able to approve your metadata. We’ll send you a ticket with the rejection details in the Resolution Center. Edit the metadata in My Apps to resolve the issue. Your submitted binary will be reused for the review process after your metadata has been fixed.
Developer Rejected: You removed your own app from the review process. The app review process will restart after you resubmit your binary.
Removed from Sale: We needed to remove your app from the App Store. To find out why your app was removed, just ask us.
Developer Removed from Sale: You removed your own app from sale on the App Store.
Pending Apple Release: We’ll hold your app version until the related iOS or OS X version is released to the public.
(Pending App Transfer): This is a secondary status, shown after the main app status. The current app owner initiated an app transfer, but the recipient hasn’t accepted it yet. Both the current app owner and the recipient have the ability to cancel the transfer. While the app has this status, no changes can be made to the app except for Rights and Pricing.

(Processing App Transfer): This is a secondary status, shown after the main app status. The recipient has accepted an app transfer. The transfer can’t be canceled at this point, and no changes can be made to the app until the transfer is complete.
